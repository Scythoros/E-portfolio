Welcome To My ASP Journey

Here you will learn of my journey through my highschool years regarding on how I was able to attain my Scholarship! You shall observe a timeline of events that showcase the many trials and obstacles I had faced to reach to where I am today. So sit back and enjoy the ride!

Where It All Started

2018 - 2019

After completing the Ninth grade, I received a document from my school advisor recommending me to apply to an Early Preparation program. I was captivated by the idea of attending summer programs abroad in the US and elsewhere, so I immediately applied and attended the intro TOEFL exam.

Early Preparation Program

2019 - 2021

The Early Preparation program served as a vital stepping stone in my academic life! It prepared me for the American educational system and opened doors to amazing opportunities.

Applying to ASP

2021 - 2022

Applying to ADIA's Scholarship program (ASP) served as the culmination of all the hard work that occurred over the span of my high school years.

Currently

2023 - Current

As of now, I am diligently working towards receiving my Bachelor's in Computer Science at Cornell University under the sponsorship of ASP!
